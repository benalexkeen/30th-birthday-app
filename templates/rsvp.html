Getting There

Hallmark Hotel Croydon Aerodrome

There is on-site parking at the Hallmark Hotel

Train

The Hallmark Hotel Croydon Aerodrome is a 15 minute walk from Waddon Train Station, a 10 minute taxi ride from East or West Croydon Train Stations, or a 5 minute taxi ride from Purley Train Station.

Bus

The Hallmark Hotel Croydon Aerodrome is served by the 119 or 289 buses. Use TfL Journey Planner for more detailed information about getting there by bus.

Aeroplane

The hotel is a 30 minute taxi ride from Gatwick Airport.

Taxi
  • Uber to Hallmark Hotel Croydon Aerodrome
  • Black Cab to Hallmark Hotel Croydon Aerodrome using Gett (requires Gett App)

About Me

1990: Birth, St Helier Hospital - The beginning of the end, in the words of Douglas Adams - "This has made a lot of people very angry and been widely regarded as a bad move".

1995: Primary School, St. Cecilia's - First responsibilities are introduced, things are downhill from here.

2001: High School, John Fisher - Responsibilities begin to grow - have to choose who my top 6 friends on Bebo will be, what my status on MSN will be, and find new and creative ways to avoid playing Rugby.

2006: 6th Form, John Fisher - Surely I should still be getting taller? Oh, no that's it.

2008: University, Southampton Uni - Racked up >20K in debt to attend a lot of fancy dress social events.

2011: Postgrad, GDSC, Sussex - Mastered the art of pipetting accurately and quickly, important life skill learnt, will certainly be using this day-to-day in the future.

2015: First Real Job, Hitachi - Sold my soul to leave cancer research and join a multinational corporation for a chance to earn some money to pay off my student loan.

2019: Second Job, Microsoft - Imposter syndrome in full swing now as I've somehow managed to con another company into thinking I'm competent at my work.

2020: Turning 30 - Seeing off my youth, first grey hairs setting in, slowing metabolism, scared by nightclubs.
